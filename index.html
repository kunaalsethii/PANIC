Rules Reference
Core Rules

Each turn you get 4 actions.

You can move, attack, treat infection, or use abilities.

Infection

Each territory can hold up to 3 infection cubes.

At 3 cubes ? outbreak happens.

Cure

3 cards = 1 cure progress.

5 progress = disease cured.

Moral Decisions

Contain ? lose troops but stop spread

Abandon ? retreat but zombies spawn

Weaponize ? create zombie zone and gain points

Regional Triggers

Outbreak ? spreads infection

Capture ? adds contamination

Abandon ? creates zombies

World System

Virus evolves over time.

Events affect all players.
